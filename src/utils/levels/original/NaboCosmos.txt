Title: Nabo Cosmos
Description: This set was published progressively on the site of Paul Voyer, Gerald Holler
             and Frantisek Pokorny. I wanted this set more difficult than Microcosmos, but
             still with small levels. And why Nabokosmos ? Read backwards "Som(e)sokoban";-)
             This set contains 2 levels "3boxes", 8 levels "4boxes", 21 levels "5boxes", 7
             levels "6boxes" and 2 levels "7boxes".
Author: Aymeric du Peloux
Email: aymeric.du-peloux@gadz.org
Website: http://membres.lycos.fr/nabokos
